Crypto is FOR GOOD

FOR GOOD DAO aims to achieve something that no other giving platform has achieved in its space. Creating stakeholder donors who advocate for a cause to win their non-profit a larger donation. By using a DAO structure and voting mechanism we are the 1st Charity DAO that uses game theory to incentivize giving on a global scale.
